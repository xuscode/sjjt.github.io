NINGBO CITY EXHIBITION HALL

Ningbo City Exhibition Hall is an urban living room that displays the development history, construction achievements and future vision of Ningbo city. It is an important foreign exchange window for Ningbo. The total construction area of the exhibition hall is 25,000 square meters and the building height is 24 meters. It is divided into 4 floors above ground and 1 floor underground. The overall exhibition is based on the theme of "World Port City, Happy Homeland". In terms of plane layout, with "planning" as the main line and the urban development track of "from the river to the sea" as the pulse, it is divided into three major sections: comprehensive exhibition area, theme exhibition area and auxiliary exhibition area. The comprehensive exhibition area includes five parts: the atrium of the preface hall, the unique Ningbo, the future Ningbo, the future cinema, and the digital sand table, showing the core content of Ningbo's long history, splendid culture, and future development planning. The theme exhibition area includes three exhibition halls: "Quality of Life", "Green Ecology" and "Smart City".
